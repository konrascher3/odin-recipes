The Big Boi Pizza

Description

A recipe for a decently sized salami-pizza. (Original recipe)

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 large eggs
  • 3 cps of shredded mozzzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/4 cup Hard Salami
  • 1/3 cup pizza sauce (low-carb if you are fancy)
  • 2 tablespoons red onion cheese
  • Jalapeno sices (if you dare)
  • 1 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon chili-oil (a must!)

STEPS

  2. In add 4 eggs to a large mixing bowl and whisk until foamy. Stir in 2 cups mozzarella cheese and 1-Â½ teaspoons Italian Seasoning.
  3. Spread out egg and cheese mixture on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet. Bake for 20 minutes, or until crust is lightly brown and firm.Spread out egg and cheese mixture on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet. Bake for 20 minutes, or until crust is lightly brown and firm.
  4. Remove cheese crust from oven and spread pizza sauce over top.
  5. Top the sauce with remaining cup of shredded cheese, hard salami, red onion, jalapeno, and garlic slices.
  6. Return pizza to the oven and bake another 10 minutes, or until the cheese on top has melted.
  7. Drizzle hot pizza with 1 tablespoon of sugar-free honey or maple syrup and chili oil if desired.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
